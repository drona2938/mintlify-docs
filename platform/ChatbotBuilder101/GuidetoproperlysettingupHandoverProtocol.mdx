### Purpose

This guide provides a clear, step-by-step process for correctly setting up Meta's chatbot's handover protocol. This needs to be done for both Messenger and Instagram DM to perform correctly.

### Procedure

#### Inputs (what you need to get started)

* Access to the Meta platform
* Page login credentials
* Basic understanding of navigating platform settings

<Steps>
    <Step>
        Log in to the Facebook platform and navigate to your business page. Be sure to be logged in as your Page, not as yourself.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Navigate to the settings by clicking on the left-hand side menu.
        <img
          src="https://gb-v2-upload-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/150000180344/handover-protocol-1.png"
          alt="Facebook Page Settings"
        />
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Since Meta recently updated its interface, locate "Page Setup" within the settings.
        <img
          src="https://gb-v2-upload-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/150000180344/handover-protocol-2.png"
          alt="Page Setup"
        />
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Within "Page Setup," find "Advanced Messaging" and click on "View" to access the handover protocol settings.
        <img
          src="https://gb-v2-upload-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/150000180344/handover-protocol-3.png"
          alt="Advanced Messaging"
        />
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Click on "Configure" if the handover protocol settings are empty. Select "ChatBot Builder" and save the changes.
        <img
          src="https://gb-v2-upload-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/150000180344/handover-protocol-4.png"
          alt="Configure Handover Protocol"
        />
    </Step>
    <Step>
        If connecting to Instagram, repeat the same process by selecting "Instagram" instead of "ChatBot Builder."
    </Step>
</Steps>

#### Outputs (what you get when you're done)

* Handover protocol configured correctly for the Meta chatbot.
* Seamless integration with ChatBot Builder or Instagram, as per preference.
