### The Flow Page

The "Flow" page is where you'll manage all your chatbot flows.

![Flow page overview](https://gb-v2-upload-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/150000055075/create-your-first-flow-1.png)

Here's a quick overview of the key elements on this page:

- **Generic Search Bar:** You can search for anything inside the platform, including flows.
- **Main Menu:** Access other tabs in the platform from the left-side menu.
- **Tags & Custom Fields:** These links will take you to the detailed list and analytics of tags and custom fields respectively.

### Creating a New Flow

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click on the **“Add Flow”** button to create a new flow for your chatbot
    campaign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### The Flow Table

The flow table provides a summary of all your flows, including:

- **Flow Name:** The name of your flow.
- **Modified Date:** The date when the flow was last modified.
- **Menu:** A menu for each flow with the following options:
  - **Preview:** Preview your flow.
  - **Analytics:** View analytics for your flow.
  - **Flow Link:** Get a shareable link to your flow.
  - **Edit:** Edit your flow.
  - **Delete:** Delete your flow.

### Deleting Flows

To delete one or more flows, select the flows you want to remove and click on the trash icon below the table.

![Deleting flows](https://gb-v2-upload-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/150000055075/create-your-first-flow-2.png)

<Note>
  The **Yellow color** before the flow name indicates that the flow is **unpublished**. It will not be shown to your subscribers until it is published.
</Note>

<Info>
  Always make sure that your flow is **published** before making it live.
</Info>
